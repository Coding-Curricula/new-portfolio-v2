// ABOUT

I’m Chris — a web developer in Texas. I build sites and apps for real businesses, and I keep this portfolio in stubborn hand-written HTML and CSS to prove the platform can still carry its own weight.

More about me »

Off the clock I’m with my family and our dogs, which is to say outside more than inside. I’m partial to the color blue — which is why this site wears it — and to The Wire, which remains the best argument that television can be literature.

I work on web apps for the San Antonio area and ship the occasional mobile app. If you’ve got something to build, I’d like to hear about it.
